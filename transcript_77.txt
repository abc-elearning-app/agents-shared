These are three free cybersecurity certifications. I wish I knew about sooner.  Number one, the ISC-2 CC. ISC-2 is one of the biggest names in cybersecurity and  the CC is equivalent to the Comp-TS Security Plus, but for free. The training and  exam are completely free. However, you do have to pay $50 per year once you pass.  Number two, the Fortnite NSC-1 and NSC-2. Fortnite is a massive cybersecurity  vendor and these will teach you threat detection and network security. These are  100% free, no strings attached, and you can pretty much dip these in around 10  hours. Number three, the Quayless VMDR. This certification will prove that you  can use real vulnerability management tools that Fortune 500 companies  actually use in production. This is also 100% free with hands-on labs that  immediately translate to entry-level soft analyst roles. I post every day so  follow for more cybersecurity tips.
