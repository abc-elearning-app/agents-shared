Mechanical Comprehension Study Guide

Page 1: Principles of Mechanical Devices & Newton's Laws

  • Mass vs Weight, Force (Vector), Gravity (F=G*m1*m2/r^2), Friction.
  • Compression and Tension forces.
  • Newton's Laws: 1 (Inertia), 2 (F=ma), 3 (Action/Reaction).
  • Fluid Dynamics: Air/Water Pressure, Hydraulic Lift (A1*D1 = A2*D2).
  • Work (F*d), Energy (KE, PE), and Power (W/t).

Page 2: Mechanical Advantage & Simple Machines

  • Mechanical Advantage (MA) formulas: MA = Load/Effort or Effort Distance/Load Distance.
  • Gears: MA = Driven teeth / Driving teeth.
  • Inclined Planes: MA = Length / Rise.
  • Levers: Class 1 (Fulcrum middle), Class 2 (Load middle), Class 3 (Effort middle).
  • Pulleys, Screws, Wedges, Wheel and Axle MA.
  • Material Properties: Conduction, Flexibility, Malleability.
