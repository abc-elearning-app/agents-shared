Word Knowledge (WK) Study Guide

  • Focus: Synonyms and Contextual meaning.
  • Question Types: Straight Definition (Synonyms) and Contextual (Sentence-based).
  • Strategies: Root Words (e.g., bene- = good), Prefixes, Suffixes (-ology = study of). decoding unfamiliar words.
  • Elimination: If two choices are identical, both are likely wrong. Use plug-in method for sentences.

Paragraph Comprehension (PC) Study Guide

  • Focus: Extracting info, Main Ideas, Drawing conclusions.
  • Question Types: Detail-Oriented, Main Idea/Global theme, Inferencing (logical conclusions).
  • Strategies: Read the Question FIRST to identify target. Watch for absolute qualifiers (always, never).
  • Verification: Refer back to the paragraph for every answer choice.
