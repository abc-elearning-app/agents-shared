Assembling Objects (AO) Study Guide

  • Focus: Spatial Reasoning (Mental rotation and connection).
  • Question Types: Connection Items (Point A to Point B) and Puzzle Items (Fitting shapes together).
  • The "No Mirroring" Rule: Pieces are rotated but NEVER flipped or mirrored. Eliminate mirrored choices.
  • Landmark Method: Pick a unique shape/dot/angle and find its position in answer choices.
  • Mental Rotation: Practice visualizing objects turning clockwise or counter-clockwise.
