General Science Study Guide

Page 1: Life Science - Human Body

  • Nutrition: Macronutrients (Carbs, Lipids, Proteins) and Micronutrients (Vitamins/Minerals).
  • Body Systems: Skeletal/Muscular, Circulatory (Heart/Arteries/Veins), Nervous (Central/Peripheral).
  • Blood Types: A, B, AB (Recipient), O (Donor), Rh Factor.

Page 2: Genetics, Cells & Ecology

  • Genetics: Genotype vs Phenotype, DNA, Punnett Square.
  • Cells: Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic, Organelles (Mitochondria, Ribosomes, Lysosomes).
  • Ecology: Biosphere, Biome, Ecosystem, Community, Population. Taxonomy hierarchy.

Page 3: Earth & Space Science

  • Geology: Earth Layers (Core, Mantle, Crust), Rock Types (Igneous, Sedimentary, Metamorphic).
  • Meteorology: Atmosphere Layers, Cloud Types (Stratus, Cumulus, Cirrus).
  • Astronomy: Solar System Planets, Solar/Lunar Eclipses.
