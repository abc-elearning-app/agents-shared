Electronics Information Study Guide

Page 1: General Concepts & DC Circuits

  • Atomic Structure: Protons (+), Neutrons (0), Electrons (-), Valence Shell.
  • Materials: Conductors (Copper), Insulators (Rubber), Semiconductors (Graphite).
  • Ohm's Law: I = V / R (Current, Voltage, Resistance).
  • Circuits: Closed vs Open, Load, Series (same current), Parallel (same voltage).
  • Power: P = V*I, P = I^2*R, P = V^2/R (Watts).

Page 2: AC Systems & Components

  • AC (Alternating) vs DC (Direct). Frequency (60 Hz in US, f=1/T).
  • Safety: Fuses and Circuit Breakers.
  • Capacitors (Farads), Diodes (Rectification: AC to DC).
  • Transistors: Three terminals (Base, Collector, Emitter) used for amplification/switching.
