ASVAB COMPREHENSIVE STUDY GUIDE - UNABRIDGED

1. Mathematics Knowledge (MK) & Arithmetic Reasoning (AR)

Core Arithmetic & Number Properties: PEMDAS (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication/Division, Addition/Subtraction). Fractions: Addition/Subtraction needs common denominator, Multiplication is direct, Division flips the second fraction. Percentages formula: Part = Percent * Whole.

Algebraic Formulas: Linear Equations y = mx + b. Slope formula m = (y2-y1)/(x2-x1). Quadratic Formula x = (-b ± sqrt(b^2-4ac))/2a. Exponents rules: x^a * x^b = x^(a+b), x^a / x^b = x^(a-b), (x^a)^b = x^(ab).

Geometry: Circle Area A = pi*r^2, Circumference C = 2*pi*r. Triangle Area A = 1/2*b*h, Pythagorean Theorem a^2+b^2=c^2. Rectangle Area A = l*w, Perimeter P = 2l+2w. Volume: Rectangular Prism V = l*w*h, Cylinder V = pi*r^2*h.

2. Mechanical Comprehension (MC)

Simple Machines: Levers (Effort * Effort Arm = Resistance * Resistance Arm). Classes: 1st (Fulcrum middle), 2nd (Load middle), 3rd (Effort middle). Pulleys: Fixed (MA=1), Movable (MA=number of ropes). Gears: Ratio of teeth determines speed vs torque. Mesh gears rotate oppositely; idler gear makes them rotate same direction.

Physical Principles: Work = Force * Distance (Joules). Power = Work / Time (Watts). Pressure = Force / Area. Newton's Laws: 1. Inertia, 2. F = ma, 3. Action/Reaction.

3. Electronics Information (EI)

Basic Units: Voltage (V) - pressure, Current (I) - flow (Amps), Resistance (R) - opposition (Ohms), Power (P) - consumption (Watts). Ohm's Law: V = I * R. Power Law: P = V * I.

Circuits: Series Circuit (single path, Rt = R1 + R2...). Parallel Circuit (multiple paths, voltage same across branches). Components: Resistors (limit current), Capacitors (store charge), Inductors (magnetic storage), Diodes (one-way valve), Transistors (switch/amplifier), Transformers (magnetic induction for AC voltage change).

4. General Science (GS)

Biology: Cell structure (Mitochondria powerhouse), Genetics (DNA), Human Systems (Circulatory, Digestive). Chemistry: Atomic structure (Protons, Neutrons, Electrons), Periodic Table, pH scale (0-6 Acid, 7 Neutral, 8-14 Base). Physics: Forms of energy (Kinetic vs Potential), Heat transfer (Conduction, Convection, Radiation).
